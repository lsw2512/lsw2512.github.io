## GingerBread martini
- 1 ½ ounce vanilla vodka
- ½ ounce kahlua or creme de cacao (for no coffee)
- ½ ounce Frangelico
- 2 ounce Baileys
- 1 ounce Gingerbread syrup

Combine in cocktail shaken then strain into glass
